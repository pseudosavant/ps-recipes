# Apple Pie

## Filling Ingredients
* 6-8 apples (2-3 lbs): Pink Lady, Gala, or Granny Smith
* 3/4 cup granulated sugar
* 1/4 cup brown sugar
* 2 tbsp flour
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 2 tbsp butter
* 1 tsp lemon juice

## Crust
* [Trader Joe's Pie Crust](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/products/pdp/pie-crusts-076715)
* Thaw at room temperature for 1.5 hours

## Crumb Topping
* 1 cup flour
* 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
* 2 tbsp granulated sugar
* 1/4 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1/4 cup oats
* 8 tbsp butter, room temperature

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Prepare the apple filling.
   1. Peel, core, and slice the apples into a large microwave-safe glass bowl.  
      An [electric apple peeler](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B094YR66JG) and a [16-slice apple cutter](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00EZQQO9Q) make this *much* faster.
   2. In a separate bowl, mix the dry pie filling ingredients.
   3. Add the butter and lemon juice to the apples, then sprinkle the dry mixture over the top.
   4. Cover the bowl tightly with plastic wrap. Optionally, poke one small vent hole in the plastic wrap with a knife.
   5. Gently shake/toss to coat the apples.
   6. Microwave for 8 minutes.
   7. Using oven mitts, carefully shake the bowl to mix the filling.
   8. Microwave for 4 more minutes.
   9. Using oven mitts, carefully shake or stir again.
3. Prepare the crumb topping.
   1. In a medium bowl, mix the dry crumb topping ingredients.
   2. Soften the room-temperature butter slightly in the microwave.
   3. Add the softened butter and mix until crumbly.
4. Place the thawed pie dough into the pie pan.
5. Carefully remove the plastic wrap from the apple filling so steam vents away from your face and hands.
6. Add the apple filling and spread it evenly.
7. Cover evenly with crumb topping.
8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the crust and crumb topping are golden brown.

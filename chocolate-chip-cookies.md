# Chocolate Chip Cookies
## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup brown sugar
* 1 stick of softened butter
* 1 egg
* 1 tsp vanilla
* 1 1/2 cup flour
* 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Roll dough into 2 tbsp balls
2. Place onto 2 cookie sheets
3. Bake both cookie sheets at 350Â° for 5 minutes on the middle and lower racks
4. Switch the cookie sheets between racks
5. Bake for 5 minutes
6. Remove from oven

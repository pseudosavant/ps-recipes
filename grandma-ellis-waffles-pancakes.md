# Grandma Ellis' Waffles/Pancakes
## Ingredients

* 2 cups flour (240g)
* 1 tsp baking soda (5g)
* 2 tsp baking powder (10g)
* 1/2 tsp salt (3g)
* 2 cups buttermilk (490g)
* 1/4 cup oil (55g)
* 2 eggs

## Instructions
1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix eggs and liquid ingredients
3. Mix liquids into dry ingredients
